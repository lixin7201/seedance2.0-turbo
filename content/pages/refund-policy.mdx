## Refund Policy

At Seedance 2.0, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for requesting a refund.

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** for all subscription plans. If you are not satisfied with our service for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team:

- **Email**: [support@seedance2.shop](mailto:support@seedance2.shop)
- **Include**: Your account email address and order/subscription ID

## Response Time

We are committed to responding to all refund requests within **3 business days**. Our team will review your request and process eligible refunds promptly.

## Refund Processing

- Refunds will be credited to your original payment method
- Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider (typically 5-10 business days)
- You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed

## After the 14-Day Period

Refund requests made after the 14-day period will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We may offer partial refunds or account credits depending on the circumstances.

## Non-Refundable Items

- Credits that have already been used for video generation
- Any promotional or discounted purchases (unless specifically stated otherwise)

## Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting our support team. Upon cancellation:

- You will retain access to your subscription benefits until the end of your current billing period
- No further charges will be made to your account
- Unused credits will expire at the end of the billing period

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this refund policy, please contact us at [support@seedance2.shop](mailto:support@seedance2.shop).

This refund policy is effective as of the date of your subscription and may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
